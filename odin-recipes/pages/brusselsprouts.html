Roasted Brussel Sprouts

Ingredients

  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Neutral Oil (Canola, Grapeseed, etc...)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 325 F and place sheetpan into oven
  2. Cut brussel spout into halves
  3. Toss brussel sprouts in oil and season with salt and pepper
  4. Take pan out of oven and place brussel sprouts cut side facing down onto sheet pan
  5. Place pan back into oven and wait 20 - 30 minutes
  6. Remove from oven and serve.
