Flash Brew Iced Coffee

Ingredient List

  • 20g Medium Grind Coffee
  • 125g Ice
  • 125g Hot Water (93 C)
  • Coffee Brewer (V60, Chemex, Aeropress, etc...)

Make Coffee According to Your Brewer - Instructions below relate to Hario Switch

  1. Rinse Filter
  2. Place coffee grinds into filter
  3. Pour 40g hot water into coffee grinds
  4. Move coffee around to make coffee grinds wet
  5. Pour the remaining 85g of hot water
  6. Wait 4 minutes
  7. Dispense coffee into cup with ice
  8. Enjoy.

The idea with this coffee is to brew as you usually do however instead of all hot water you are halving the water and using ice for the other half. This produces an iced coffee that will still have the more subtle, fruity, floral, etc. notes of a regular brewed coffee but now is in an iced form. Also because the ice is considered part of the water in the brewing process the coffee will not be diluted as if you added ice cubes to a regularly brewed coffee.
