Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. At least 2 hours before, preferably the night before, salt steak and let rest in frige on wire rack
  2. Add steak into cold pan and heat on medium
  3. Once steak releases from pan flip steak and sear other side
  4. Flip steak every 15 seconds or so until done
  5. Remove from pan and let steak rest at least 5 minutes before cutting and serving

Salting the steak the night before allows the steak to dry out as well as absorb salt into meat seasoning it. Starting the steak in a cold pan with no oil allows the steaks fat to render which the steak will cook itself in. Flipping the steak every 15 seconds will cook the steak faster, preventing the outside from burning.
